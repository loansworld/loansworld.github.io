Top 10 Lenders for Personal Loans in Johnson City, TN

Home / Blog / Blog Details

When it comes to securing a personal loan in Johnson City, TN, the options can feel overwhelming. Whether you're looking to consolidate debt, cover unexpected medical bills, or finance a home improvement project, choosing the right lender is crucial. In today’s economic climate—marked by rising inflation, fluctuating interest rates, and post-pandemic financial recovery—finding a reliable and affordable loan provider is more important than ever.

To help you navigate the lending landscape, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 lenders for personal loans in Johnson City, TN, highlighting their key features, eligibility requirements, and how they stack up in today’s competitive market.

1. Eastman Credit Union (ECU)

Why Choose ECU?

As one of the largest credit unions in Tennessee, ECU offers competitive rates and flexible terms for personal loans. Their member-focused approach makes them a standout choice, especially for those who prefer a community-driven financial institution.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 6.99% – 18.00%
  • Loan Amounts: $500 – $50,000
  • Repayment Terms: Up to 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 650+ (varies)

Best For:

Borrowers with good credit who want low-interest rates and personalized service.

2. Bank of Tennessee

Why Choose Bank of Tennessee?

A trusted local bank, Bank of Tennessee provides personal loans with transparent terms and no hidden fees. Their digital application process is streamlined for convenience.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 7.50% – 19.99%
  • Loan Amounts: $1,000 – $35,000
  • Repayment Terms: 12 – 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 620+

Best For:

Those who prefer in-person banking and want a straightforward loan process.

3. LightStream (Online Lender)

Why Choose LightStream?

LightStream, a division of Truist Bank, is known for its low rates and same-day funding for qualified borrowers. They cater to individuals with excellent credit.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 5.99% – 23.99% (with autopay discount)
  • Loan Amounts: $5,000 – $100,000
  • Repayment Terms: 24 – 84 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 720+

Best For:

High-credit borrowers seeking large loan amounts and fast funding.

4. Upstart (AI-Powered Lending)

Why Choose Upstart?

Upstart uses artificial intelligence to evaluate borrowers beyond just credit scores, making it a great option for those with limited credit history.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 5.20% – 35.99%
  • Loan Amounts: $1,000 – $50,000
  • Repayment Terms: 36 – 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 580+

Best For:

Young professionals or recent graduates with strong income potential but limited credit.

5. LendingClub (Peer-to-Peer Lending)

Why Choose LendingClub?

LendingClub connects borrowers with individual investors, often resulting in competitive rates for those with fair to good credit.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 8.05% – 36.00%
  • Loan Amounts: $1,000 – $40,000
  • Repayment Terms: 36 – 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 600+

Best For:

Borrowers who want an alternative to traditional banks.

6. SoFi (Online Lender)

Why Choose SoFi?

SoFi offers unemployment protection, career coaching, and member-exclusive perks alongside personal loans.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 8.99% – 25.81%
  • Loan Amounts: $5,000 – $100,000
  • Repayment Terms: 24 – 84 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 680+

Best For:

High-earning professionals looking for additional financial benefits.

7. Avant (For Fair Credit Borrowers)

Why Choose Avant?

Avant specializes in loans for borrowers with fair credit (600–700), offering quick approvals and funding.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 9.95% – 35.99%
  • Loan Amounts: $2,000 – $35,000
  • Repayment Terms: 12 – 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 580+

Best For:

Those with less-than-perfect credit who need fast cash.

8. OneMain Financial (Secured & Unsecured Loans)

Why Choose OneMain Financial?

OneMain Financial provides both secured and unsecured loans, making it accessible for borrowers with varying credit profiles.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 18.00% – 35.99%
  • Loan Amounts: $1,500 – $20,000
  • Repayment Terms: 24 – 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: None (but higher rates for lower credit)

Best For:

Borrowers who may need collateral to secure a loan.

9. Discover Personal Loans

Why Choose Discover?

Discover offers no origination fees and a 30-day money-back guarantee if you change your mind.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 7.99% – 24.99%
  • Loan Amounts: $2,500 – $40,000
  • Repayment Terms: 36 – 84 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 660+

Best For:

Borrowers who value transparency and customer service.

10. Payoff (Debt Consolidation Focused)

Why Choose Payoff?

Payoff specializes in debt consolidation loans, helping borrowers reduce credit card debt efficiently.

Loan Features

  • APR Range: 8.99% – 29.99%
  • Loan Amounts: $5,000 – $40,000
  • Repayment Terms: 24 – 60 months
  • Credit Score Requirement: 640+

Best For:

Individuals looking to streamline high-interest credit card debt.

Final Considerations Before Applying

Check Your Credit Score

Knowing your credit score beforehand helps you target lenders that match your financial profile.

Compare Rates & Fees

Some lenders charge origination fees (1%–8%), while others offer fee-free loans.

Read Reviews & Testimonials

Customer experiences can reveal hidden pros and cons of each lender.

Pre-Qualify When Possible

Many lenders offer soft credit checks that don’t impact your score.

With the right lender, a personal loan can be a powerful financial tool—whether you're tackling debt, handling emergencies, or investing in your future. Research thoroughly, compare options, and choose wisely!

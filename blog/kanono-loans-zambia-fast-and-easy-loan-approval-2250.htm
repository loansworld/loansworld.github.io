Responsible Borrowing: Tips for Loan Applicants

While Kanono Loans Zambia makes borrowing easy, it’s essential to practice financial responsibility. Here are some tips:

Borrow Only What You Need

It can be tempting to take out a larger loan than necessary, but remember—every loan must be repaid with interest.

Understand the Terms

Before signing any agreement, ensure you fully comprehend the interest rates, fees, and repayment schedule.

Plan Your Repayments

Defaulting on a loan can harm your credit score. Set reminders or automate payments to avoid missing due dates.
